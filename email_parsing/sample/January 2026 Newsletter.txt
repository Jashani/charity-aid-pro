Building Capacity, Creating Change for Thanet




On Wednesday 28th January, our Charity Chief Officer, Kerry Donati, had the honour of attending and presenting at the Thanet Community Champions Conference in Broadstairs - a brilliant celebration of the inspiring projects delivered through the UK Shared Prosperity Fund in Thanet.

During the morning, Kerry shared how funding received through the Thanet Community Champions Programme enabled Funding for All to provide dedicated funding support to VCFSEs across the district. Through our mentoring programme, we’ve been able to help organisations build long‑term fundraising capacity and deliver truly transformative change.

Between 2024 and March 2025:


*       82 VCFSE organisations accessed our support
*       31 groups received one‑to‑one mentoring
*       51 groups accessed funding advice and training
*       213.75 hours of funded support were delivered


These groups secured a combined £622,278 from multiple funding sources!
This means that for every £1 invested by Thanet District Council, £26 was brought back into the district - a return on investment we’re incredibly proud of.

We’re honoured to be part of the UK Shared Prosperity Fund delivery in Thanet, working alongside Thanet District Council and fantastic local partners. It was especially inspiring to hear directly from groups who have benefitted from the fund and to see TDC launch their UK Shared Prosperity Fund for Thanet Impact Report.
