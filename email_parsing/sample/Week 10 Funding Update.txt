Alongside finding 66 new grants this week (link at the bottom) we’ve also released some pretty huge updates to the platform.

The free database has been upgraded.
You can now see detailed funder descriptions, clear eligibility information, and direct links to apply.

Tenders are now live.
You can now find and draft government tenders and contracts inside Fundin.

Grant matching has been improved.
Our matching now takes into account more information about your organisation, including income, trustees, location and structure.

You can now add your own grants and tenders.
If you already know about opportunities you want to apply for, you can add them directly into Fundin and still use the AI drafting and management tools.

Sources added to AI drafts.
You can now see where information in your drafts is coming from, making it easier to review, verify and strengthen applications before submitting.


Multi-user is here.
You can now add your team and collaborate properly. No more shared logins or copying answers between documents. Just one workspace for your organisation.


If you haven’t logged in for a while, now’s a good time to take another look.
